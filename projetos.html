Projects with Power BI:

Interactive Dashboard for Financial Analysis

Goal: Dashboard in Power BI to monitor the evolution of income accounts with an emphasis on income and expenses

Result:Interactive visualizations with revenue evolution per year, YoY comparison and category and account nature filters



Quote board

Goal: Dashboard in Power BI to monitor the evolution of the dollar and the euro. And have a history of these and twenty other currencies.

Result: Interactive visualization of the evolution of the monthly average and final count of the dollar and euro, with monthly and quarterly views. In addition to tables with the conversion value of the twenty-two currencies.



Sales performance dashboard

Goal: Dashboard in Power BI to monitor sales results and costs by product and region. And check the results of the promotions.

Result: the creation of visuals to highlight the evolution of sales and costs, the quantity sold per promotion and per product, as well as margin per category, ranking of best sellers. Revenue, Cost and Margin can be filtered by region.



Projects with Pentaho:

ETL flow with pentaho

Goal: Three-Table Merge and Database Insertion.

Result: ETL flow with pentaho that generates a database, based on information from three different tables. The flow will be executed by a job, also created in pentaho, which will send an email with the execution status and create a log file. This way we guarantee the diligence of the process and the continuous updating of data.



Projects with Python:

Exploratory HR Data Analysis.

Exploratory HR Data Analysis

Metodology: Detalhe as etapas do projeto, utilizando Dataset com dados fictícios de RH, foi realizada uma análise exploratória com a utilização do Pandas para manipulação de dados, a utilizando matplotlib e seaborn para visualização de dados. Foi realizado um modelo de classificação para prever a possibilidade de promoção de um colaborador. O modelo escolhido foi o Logistic regression. Os dados foram tratados para evitar viés uma vez que a variável dependente estar desbalanceada. A acurácia atingida foi de 66%.
